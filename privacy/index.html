3. Contact Lists and Recipient Data

When you upload a contact list to the Tunnelo SU platform, you are uploading third-party personal data (phone numbers and names of your recipients). As the data controller for that data, you are responsible for ensuring you have a lawful basis to process that data and to send communications to those individuals.

Tunnelo processes recipient data solely to deliver the messages you instruct us to send. We do not use recipient contact data for any purpose other than message delivery and delivery reporting. We do not sell or share recipient data with any third party except as required to route messages through mobile network operators.

You must ensure that:

  • You have obtained consent from recipients or have another valid legal basis to contact them.
  • Your messages comply with applicable communications laws and regulations in the recipients' jurisdictions.
  • You honour opt-out requests from recipients promptly.

4. Payment Data

Payments on the Tunnelo SU platform are processed by third-party payment processors. We do not store full payment card numbers on our systems. The following billing data is retained:

  • Transaction records (amount, date, reference) for accounting and legal compliance.
  • Billing name and contact information for invoice purposes.

Our payment processors are subject to their own privacy policies and PCI-DSS compliance requirements. By making a payment, you acknowledge that your payment data will be processed by these providers.

5. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • SMS and USSD network partners: To deliver messages, recipient numbers and message content are passed to SMS gateway and telecom network partners. These partners are contractually bound to use the data only for delivery purposes.
  • Payment processors: Billing information is shared with our payment provider to process transactions.
  • Cloud infrastructure providers: Our platform runs on cloud infrastructure where your data may be stored and processed. These providers operate under data processing agreements.
  • Legal obligations: Where required by law, court order, or a request from a regulatory authority, we may disclose personal data to comply with our legal obligations.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of Tunnelo's business, personal data may be transferred to the successor entity under equivalent data protection obligations.

6. Data Retention

  • Contact form enquiries: Retained for 12 months from the date of submission, then deleted unless we have entered into a client relationship.
  • Platform account data: Retained for the duration of your active account and for 2 years after account closure for legal and audit purposes.
  • SMS campaign data and delivery logs: Retained for 12 months from the date of the campaign.
  • Contact lists uploaded to the platform: Retained until you delete them or close your account.
  • Payment and transaction records: Retained for 7 years to comply with Ghanaian financial record-keeping requirements.
  • Custom software project data: Retained for the duration of the project and for 5 years thereafter.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, destruction, or alteration, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) across all platform endpoints.
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorised personnel only.
  • Regular security reviews of our systems and infrastructure.
  • Confidentiality obligations for all team members who handle personal data.

No system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users and the relevant authorities as required by law.

8. Your Rights

Under the Ghana Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843), you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: You can ask us to delete your personal data where there is no longer a lawful basis for us to retain it.
  • Right to restrict processing: You can ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You can object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@tunnelo.click. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission of Ghana.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies essential to its operation. We do not use advertising trackers, third-party analytics cookies, or cross-site tracking technologies.

The Tunnelo SU platform uses session cookies to keep you logged in during your session. These are deleted when you close your browser or log out.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately at hello@tunnelo.click and we will delete it promptly.

11. International Data Transfers

Where personal data is transferred to cloud infrastructure or third-party service providers outside Ghana, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place — including contractual data processing agreements — to provide an equivalent level of data protection.

Your continued use of our services after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
